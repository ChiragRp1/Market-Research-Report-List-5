<p><p>High-Density Packaging Market Types include MCM, MCP, SIP, and 3D-TSV Packaging Techniques. MCM or Multi-Chip Module packaging involves assembling multiple integrated circuits on a single substrate. MCP or Multi-Chip Package techniques stack chips vertically to reduce footprint. SIP or System-in-Package techniques integrate multiple chips into a single package. 3D-TSV Packaging Techniques involve stacking and connecting chips through Through-Silicon Vias. These packaging techniques enable increased functionality, performance, and miniaturization of electronic devices, catering to the growing demand for compact, high-performance products.</p></p>
