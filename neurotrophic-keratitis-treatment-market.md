<p><p>Neurotrophic keratitis is a rare degenerative disease of the cornea caused by damage to the trigeminal nerve, leading to decreased or complete loss of corneal sensitivity. Treatment for neurotrophic keratitis aims to promote corneal healing, reduce pain, and prevent complications such as corneal scarring and ulcers. Common treatment options include preservative-free artificial tears, ointments, bandage contact lenses, autologous serum eye drops, neurotrophic growth factors, and surgical interventions such as amniotic membrane transplantation.</p><p>The Neurotrophic Keratitis Treatment Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.7% during the forecast period.
