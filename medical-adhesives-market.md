<p><p>Medical adhesives can be classified into three main types based on their composition: water-based, solvent-based, and solid & hot melt adhesives. Water-based adhesives are environmentally friendly and easily removable, making them ideal for sensitive applications. Solvent-based adhesives offer strong bonding capabilities and rapid drying times. Solid & hot melt adhesives are made of solid materials that soften when heated, creating a strong bond upon cooling. Each type of medical adhesive has specific advantages and applications within the healthcare industry.</p></p>
