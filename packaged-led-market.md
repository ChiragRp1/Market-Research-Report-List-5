<p><ul><li>Visible LED</li><li>IR LED</li><li>UV LED</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Packaged LED market includes three main types: Visible LED, IR LED, and UV LED. Visible LED is widely used in applications like displays, signage, and automotive lighting due to its ability to produce light that is visible to the human eye. IR LED is employed in devices like remote controls, security cameras, and sensors since it emits infrared light that is invisible to humans but useful for communication and detection. UV LED finds applications in medical sterilization, water purification, and curing processes due to its ability to emit ultraviolet light that can disinfect and bond materials.</p></p>
