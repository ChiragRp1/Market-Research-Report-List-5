<p><p>VOC Treatment & Recovery markets offer various technologies for controlling volatile organic compound emissions. Regenerative Thermal Oxidation utilizes high temperatures to oxidize VOCs. Recuperative Thermal Oxidation uses heat exchange to preheat incoming process air. Catalytic Oxidation relies on catalysts to facilitate VOC combustion. Photo-oxidation utilizes UV light to break down VOCs. Adsorption by Activated Carbon traps VOCs on a filter. Rotor Concentrators rotate beds of zeolite to concentrate VOCs. Cryocondensation condenses VOCs at low temperatures. Other markets include Biofiltration and Biological Scrubbers.</p></p>
