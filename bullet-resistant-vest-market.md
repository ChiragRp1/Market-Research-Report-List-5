<p><p>Bullet resistant vests can be categorized into two main types: soft armor and hard armor. Soft armor vests are made of flexible materials such as Kevlar and provide protection against handguns and knives. They are lightweight and comfortable to wear. On the other hand, hard armor vests are constructed with rigid materials such as ceramic or metal plates, offering protection against higher caliber bullets and rifle rounds. They are heavier and bulkier than soft armor vests but provide superior levels of protection.</p></p>
