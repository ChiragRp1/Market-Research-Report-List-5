<p><p>Electrostatic Discharge Foam Packaging caters to three main market types: Metal, Conductive, and Dissipative Polymer. Metal foam packaging is made of materials like aluminum or steel and provides a high level of protection against electrostatic discharge. Conductive foam packaging contains materials that allow electrical charges to flow through easily, preventing static buildup. Dissipative Polymer foam packaging uses polymers designed to slowly discharge static electricity, reducing the risk of damage to sensitive electronic components. Each type offers varying levels of protection against electrostatic discharge.</p></p>
