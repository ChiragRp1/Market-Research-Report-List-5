<p><p>Fixed crash barrier systems are designed to prevent vehicles from leaving the road or colliding with obstacles. There are four main types of fixed crash barriers - Rigid barriers, Semi-Rigid barriers, Flexible barriers, and others. Rigid barriers are made of concrete or metal and offer maximum protection but can cause significant damage to vehicles upon impact. Semi-Rigid barriers provide a balance between protection and flexibility. Flexible barriers, such as cable barriers, absorb impact energy and reduce the risk of injury. Other types of barriers include water-filled barriers or composite materials.</p></p>
