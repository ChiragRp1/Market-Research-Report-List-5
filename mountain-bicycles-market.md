<p><p>Mountain bicycles come in various market types based on their suspension systems. Rigid bikes have no suspension and are better suited for smooth trails. Hardtail bikes feature front suspension forks for improved comfort and control on rough terrains. Softail bikes have a small amount of rear suspension to absorb bumps without the weight of a full suspension system. Full suspension bikes feature both front and rear suspension for maximum comfort and control on challenging trails. Each market type caters to different preferences and riding styles.</p></p>
