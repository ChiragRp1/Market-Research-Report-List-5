<p><ul><li>Low Level</li><li>High Level</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mechanical palletizers are classified into two main types: low level and high level palletizers. Low level palletizers are designed to operate close to the ground, making it easier to load pallets manually. They are ideal for smaller production lines with lower throughput. On the other hand, high level palletizers are designed to operate at a higher level, allowing for automated pallet loading and higher throughput. They are typically used in larger production facilities with higher production volumes. Both types of palletizers offer efficient and reliable palletizing solutions for various industries.</p></p>
