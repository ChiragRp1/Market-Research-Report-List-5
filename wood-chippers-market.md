<p><p>The Wood Chippers market can be categorized into Self-Propelled Wood Chipper and PTO Wood Chipper types. Self-Propelled Wood Chippers are equipped with their own power source and are suitable for heavy-duty industrial applications. On the other hand, PTO Wood Chippers are designed to be attached to a tractor using a power take-off (PTO) shaft, making them more versatile and suitable for smaller-scale operations. Both types cater to different user needs and preferences in the wood chipping industry.</p></p>
