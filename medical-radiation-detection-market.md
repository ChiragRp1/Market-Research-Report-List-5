<p><strong>The Medical Radiation Detection Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gas-Filled Detectors</li><li>Scintillators</li><li>Solid-State Detectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical radiation detection market consists of three main types of detectors: gas-filled detectors, scintillators, and solid-state detectors. Gas-filled detectors use gas to detect radiation and are commonly used in devices such as Geiger-Mueller counters. Scintillators use materials that emit light when exposed to radiation, allowing for detection. Solid-state detectors use semiconductor materials to directly convert radiation into electrical signals. Each type of detector has its own advantages and is used in different medical applications for detecting and measuring radiation levels.</p></p>
