<p><strong>The Reverse Thruster Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cascade Thrust Reverser</li><li>Baffle Thrust Reverser</li><li>Blocker-door Thrust Reverser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Reverse Thruster Market Types are Cascade Thrust Reverser, Baffle Thrust Reverser, and Blocker-door Thrust Reverser. Cascade Thrust Reversers redirect exhaust gases through a series of vanes to create reverse thrust. Baffle Thrust Reversers use a series of panels that extend into the exhaust stream to redirect airflow. Blocker-door Thrust Reversers have a blocker door that swings into the exhaust stream to divert airflow. Each type offers different mechanisms for achieving reverse thrust in aircraft engines.</p></p>
