<p><strong>The Pipe Relining Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cured-in-place</li><li>Pull-in-place</li><li>Pipe Bursting</li><li>Internal Pipe Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pipe relining is a method used to repair damaged or aging pipelines without the need for costly excavation. There are several types of pipe relining methods, including cured-in-place, pull-in-place, pipe bursting, and internal pipe coating. Cured-in-place involves inserting a liner and curing it in place with heat or UV light. Pull-in-place involves pulling a liner through the existing pipe and inflating it to create a new pipe inside the old one. Pipe bursting involves inserting a new pipe while breaking apart the old one. Internal pipe coating involves applying a protective coating inside the existing pipe.</p></p>
