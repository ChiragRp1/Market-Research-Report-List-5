<p><p>Non-coating grade titanium dioxide market can be divided into two major types based on the production process: sulfate process and chloride process. The sulfate process involves using sulfuric acid to extract titanium dioxide from minerals, resulting in a lower cost but lower quality product. On the other hand, the chloride process uses chlorine as a reactant, resulting in higher quality titanium dioxide suitable for various applications but at a higher cost. Both processes cater to different market demands and have their own advantages and disadvantages.</p></p>
