<p><p>Automotive heat treatment furnaces are categorized into two main types: vertical type and horizontal type. Vertical type furnaces are designed with a vertical chamber orientation where the workpieces are loaded from the top, allowing for easy loading and unloading of heavy components. On the other hand, horizontal type furnaces have a horizontal chamber orientation where the workpieces are loaded from the front, enabling efficient heat treatment of long and bulky parts. Both types cater to specific needs of the automotive industry, offering versatility and flexibility in heat treatment processes.</p></p>
