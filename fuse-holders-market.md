<p><ul><li>Open Fuse Holder</li><li>Fully Enclosed Fuse Holder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fuse holders are electrical components used to hold and protect fuses in circuits. Open fuse holders are typically made of a non-insulating material and allow easy access to the fuse for replacement. Fully enclosed fuse holders, on the other hand, provide more protection as they have a cover or casing that encloses the fuse completely. The choice between open and fully enclosed fuse holders depends on the level of protection and accessibility required for the specific circuit application.</p></p>
