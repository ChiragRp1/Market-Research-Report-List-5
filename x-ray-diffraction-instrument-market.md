<p><strong>The X-Ray Diffraction Instrument Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Powder XRD</li><li>Single-crystal XRD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>X-ray Diffraction Instrument Market includes two main types: Powder XRD and Single-crystal XRD. Powder XRD is used to determine the overall crystal structure of a material by analyzing the diffraction pattern of a powdered sample. It is commonly used for analyzing polycrystalline materials. Single-crystal XRD, on the other hand, is used to determine the precise atomic arrangement of a single crystal sample. It is often used for studying small molecules and complex crystal structures with high precision. Both types play crucial roles in materials science, chemistry, and physics research.</p></p>
