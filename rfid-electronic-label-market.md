<p><p>RFID Electronic Label Market can be categorized into different types based on their frequency bands. LF RFID operates at low frequencies, HF RFID at high frequencies, UHF RFID at ultra-high frequencies, and MW RFID at microwave frequencies. LF RFID is suitable for short-range applications, HF RFID for mid-range applications, UHF RFID for long-range applications, and MW RFID for high-speed data transfer applications. Each type of RFID technology offers unique features and benefits catering to diverse applications across various industries.</p></p>
