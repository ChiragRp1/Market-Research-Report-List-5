<p><ul><li>CO2 Type</li><li>Solid State Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plastics Laser Marking Equipment Market Types include CO2 Type and Solid State Type. CO2 Type laser marking equipment uses a carbon dioxide laser beam to mark the surface of the plastic material. It is commonly used for marking non-metal materials. On the other hand, Solid State Type laser marking equipment uses solid-state lasers like fiber, diode-pumped, or ytterbium lasers to mark plastics. These types are preferred for their high precision and efficiency in marking various plastic materials.</p></p>
