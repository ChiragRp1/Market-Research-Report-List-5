<p><p>Electrical Discharge Machining (EDM) is a machining method that uses electrical discharges to erode material from a workpiece. The three main types of EDM are Sinker EDM, Wire EDM, and Hole Drilling EDM. Sinker EDM uses a shaped electrode to create a negative of the desired shape in the workpiece. Wire EDM uses a thin wire as the electrode to cut through the workpiece. Hole Drilling EDM is used for creating small holes in the workpiece with high accuracy.</p></p>
