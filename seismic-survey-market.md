<p><p>Seismic survey market includes various types such as 2D imaging, 3D imaging, and 4D imaging. 2D imaging involves capturing two-dimensional images of the subsurface to determine the geological structure. 3D imaging provides a more detailed view by creating three-dimensional models of the subsurface. 4D imaging adds the element of time to the equation, allowing for monitoring of changes in the reservoir over time. These different types of imaging techniques help in better understanding subsurface geology and reservoirs for efficient hydrocarbon exploration.</p></p>
