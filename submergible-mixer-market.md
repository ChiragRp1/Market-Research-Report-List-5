<p><p>Submergible mixers can be classified into two main market types based on their power supply: monophase and threephase. Monophase mixers are designed to operate on single-phase electrical systems, making them suitable for residential and small commercial applications. On the other hand, threephase mixers are designed for industrial settings and larger-scale applications, as they can operate on three-phase electrical systems, providing higher power output and efficiency. The choice between monophase and threephase mixers depends on the specific requirements and scale of the mixing operation.</p></p>
