<p><strong>The Long Lasting Hair Color Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Permanent Type</li><li>Semi-permanent Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Long lasting hair color market consists of two main types: Permanent and Semi-permanent. Permanent hair color penetrates the hair cuticle and remains in the hair shaft, providing long-lasting color until new hair growth. On the other hand, Semi-permanent hair color deposits color on the outer layer of the hair shaft and gradually fades with each wash. While Permanent hair color offers more color options and coverage for gray hair, Semi-permanent hair color is gentler and easier to change.</p></p>
