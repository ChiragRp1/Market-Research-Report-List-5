<p><p>Conductive tapes market consists of two main types: Double Coated Tapes and Single Coated Tapes. Double Coated Tapes have adhesive on both sides, allowing for strong bonding between two surfaces. They are commonly used in applications requiring high conductivity. Single Coated Tapes have adhesive on just one side, making them suitable for applications where one surface needs to be insulated. Both types of tapes are used in industries such as electronics, automotive, and healthcare for their conductivity and adhesion properties.</p></p>
