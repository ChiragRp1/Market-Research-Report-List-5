<p><p>Cyclocomputer market is divided into three main types: Wired Computer, Wireless Computer, and Wireless & GPS Computer. Wired computers are connected to the bike's wheel sensor via a cable, providing basic ride data. Wireless computers use radio signals to transmit data between the sensor and the display unit, offering more flexibility in installation. Wireless & GPS computers not only track ride data wirelessly but also incorporate GPS technology for navigation and mapping capabilities, providing more advanced features for cyclists.</p></p>
