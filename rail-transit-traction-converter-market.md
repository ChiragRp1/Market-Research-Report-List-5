<p><strong>The Rail Transit Traction Converter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Propulsion Converter</li><li>Integrated Compact Converter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rail Transit Traction Converter market includes two main types: Propulsion Converter and Integrated Compact Converter. Propulsion converters are used in electric trains to control the power supply to the traction motors, helping in efficient acceleration and deceleration. Integrated Compact Converters are a combination of various functions like auxiliary power supply, braking energy recovery, and traction motor control in a single compact unit. They provide a more streamlined and space-saving solution for power conversion in rail transit systems.</p></p>
