<p><p>Effect pigments can be categorized into two main types: natural mineral pigments and artificial mineral pigments. Natural mineral pigments are derived from naturally occurring minerals and are often considered more environmentally friendly. They provide unique colors and effects to various products. On the other hand, artificial mineral pigments are synthetically produced in laboratories to mimic the properties of natural mineral pigments. Both types of pigments are widely used in industries like cosmetics, automotive, and coatings to create eye-catching visual effects.</p></p>
