<p><p>X-ray tubes are used in medical imaging to generate X-rays for diagnostic purposes. There are two main types of X-ray tubes: stationary anode and rotating anode. Stationary anode X-ray tubes are simpler in design and less expensive, making them suitable for basic imaging applications. Rotating anode X-ray tubes are more advanced and can handle higher power levels, making them ideal for more demanding imaging procedures such as CT scans. Each type of X-ray tube has its own advantages and is chosen based on the specific imaging needs.</p></p>
