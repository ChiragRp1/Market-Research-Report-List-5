<p><ul><li>Asynchronous</li><li>Synchronous</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Iron core linear motors can be categorized into two main types - asynchronous and synchronous. In asynchronous linear motors, the primary and secondary elements do not move at the same speed, which can lead to variations in performance. On the other hand, synchronous linear motors ensure that the primary and secondary elements move in sync, resulting in more accurate and efficient operation. Both types have their own advantages and are used in various applications depending on the specific requirements of the system.</p></p>
