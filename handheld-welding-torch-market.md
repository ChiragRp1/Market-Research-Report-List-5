<p><p>Handheld welding torches are essential tools used in various welding processes. Two common types of handheld welding torches are MIG/MAG welding torches and TIG welding torches. MIG/MAG welding torches are used in metal inert gas/metal active gas welding processes, which are suitable for welding steel, aluminum, and other metals. On the other hand, TIG welding torches are used in tungsten inert gas welding processes, which are preferred for high-quality welds on thin materials and exotic metals. Each type of welding torch has unique features and applications in the welding industry.</p></p>
