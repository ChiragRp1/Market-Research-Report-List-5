<p><p>Thermally-Modified Timber (TMT) is a type of wood that has undergone a heat treatment process to improve its durability and stability. There are different types of TMT available in the market, such as Thermo-S and Thermo-D. Thermo-S TMT is treated at a lower temperature for a shorter period, resulting in a lighter color and softer surface. Thermo-D TMT, on the other hand, is treated at a higher temperature for a longer period, leading to a darker color and harder surface. These variations cater to different preferences and usage requirements in the construction industry.</p></p>
