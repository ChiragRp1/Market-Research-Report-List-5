<p><p>The Railway Antifriction Bearing Market includes two main types: Spherical and Cylindrical bearings. Spherical bearings have a rounded outer ring that allows for misalignment and tilting movements, ideal for applications where shaft deflection or misalignment is common. Cylindrical bearings have a cylindrical shape and are best suited for applications with high radial loads and limited space. Both types are commonly used in railway systems to reduce friction and maintain smooth operation, ensuring efficient and reliable performance of trains and other rolling stock.</p></p>
