<p><p>Pot bearings are commonly used in bridges and structures to allow for movement and rotation. There are three main types of pot bearing markets: free sliding pot bearings allow for horizontal movement, fixed pot bearings restrict movement in all directions, and guided pot bearings allow for movement in specific directions. Each type of pot bearing serves a specific purpose depending on the design and requirements of the structure, providing support and flexibility while ensuring stability and safety.</p></p>
