<p><ul><li>Gear-Driven Side Entry Mixer</li><li>Belt Driven Side-Entry Mixer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gear-Driven Side Entry Mixer is a type of heavy-duty agitator that uses gears to drive the shaft and blades, providing high torque and efficiency for mixing viscous liquids or suspensions. On the other hand, Belt-Driven Side-Entry Mixer uses belts to transfer power from the motor to the shaft, offering a cost-effective solution for moderate to heavy mixing applications. Both types are commonly used in industries such as chemical processing, oil & gas, and wastewater treatment for large-scale agitation requirements.</p></p>
