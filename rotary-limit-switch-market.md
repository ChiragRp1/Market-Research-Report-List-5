<p><p>The Rotary Limit Switch market consists of two main types: Gear Type and Encoded Type. Gear Type switches are mechanical devices that use gears and cams to control the limit positions of machinery. Encoded Type switches use electronic sensors to track the position of a rotating shaft. Gear Type switches are often used in heavy-duty industrial applications, while Encoded Type switches are more commonly found in precision machinery where accuracy is crucial. Both types play a vital role in ensuring the safe and efficient operation of rotating equipment.</p></p>
