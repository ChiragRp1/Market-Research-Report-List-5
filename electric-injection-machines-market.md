These machines are ideal for applications where space is limited and for producing small to medium-sized parts. Horizontal injection machines have a horizontal orientation, making them suitable for producing larger parts and for applications requiring a high level of precision. Both types of machines are used in a wide range of industries, including automotive, electronics, and medical.</p></p>
