<p><p>Transient Voltage Surge Suppressors (TVSS) are devices designed to protect electrical and electronic equipment from voltage spikes or surges. TVSS devices work by diverting excess voltage away from equipment, ensuring that it receives a steady and safe supply of electricity.</p><p>The market for TVSS devices is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.5% during the forecast period.
