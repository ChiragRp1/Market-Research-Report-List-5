<p><p>Blister lidding foils are categorized based on their thickness, with three main types: below 20 μm, 20 μm-25 μm, and 25 μm-30 μm. Foils below 20 μm are lightweight and offer high barrier properties, making them suitable for protecting pharmaceutical products. Foils in the 20 μm-25 μm range provide a balance of strength and flexibility, ideal for a wide range of blister packaging applications. Foils in the 25 μm-30 μm range offer increased durability and protection, commonly used for heavier or more sensitive products.</p></p>
