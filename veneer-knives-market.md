<p><p>Veneer knives are specialized cutting tools used in the woodworking industry for slicing, clipping, and peeling operations. Slicing knives are designed for cutting veneers into thin sheets, while clipper knives are used for trimming edges. Peeling knives are used to remove the outer layer of wood from a log. Other types of veneer knives may include guillotine knives, veneer lathe knives, and rotary knives, each serving a specific purpose in the processing of wood veneers.</p></p>
