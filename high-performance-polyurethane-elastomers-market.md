<p><p>High Performance Polyurethane Elastomers are a versatile material used in various industries due to their exceptional properties. There are two main types of markets in the industry: Thermoset PU Elastomers and Thermoplastic PU Elastomers. Thermoset PU Elastomers have crosslinked molecular structures, providing excellent chemical and heat resistance. On the other hand, Thermoplastic PU Elastomers have reversible crosslinks, allowing them to be melted and reformed, making them suitable for recyclability and easy processing. Both types offer high performance and durability in different applications.</p></p>
