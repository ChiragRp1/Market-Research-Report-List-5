<p><p>PPS Filaments Market Types encompass Multifilament and Monofilament markets. Multifilament PPS filaments consist of multiple thin filaments woven together to form a strong and durable material, making them ideal for applications requiring high tensile strength. On the other hand, Monofilament PPS filaments are single-strand filaments known for their flexibility and smooth texture, suitable for applications where precision and high surface quality are crucial. Both types offer unique properties and applications within the PPS filaments market.</p></p>
