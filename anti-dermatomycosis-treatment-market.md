<p><p>Anti-Dermatomycosis Treatment Market is categorized into different types based on their mechanism of action. Polyenes work by binding to ergosterol in the fungal cell membrane, disrupting its structure. Azoles inhibit the synthesis of ergosterol, a key component of fungal cell membranes. Allylamines block the enzyme involved in ergosterol synthesis. Echinocandins inhibit the synthesis of beta-glucan, a vital component of the fungal cell wall. Other treatments include topical antifungals and combination therapies. Each type of treatment offers unique benefits in treating dermatomycosis.</p></p>
