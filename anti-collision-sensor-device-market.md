<p><p>Anti-collision sensor devices come in various types such as radar, cameras, ultrasound, and LiDAR. Radar uses radio waves to detect surrounding objects, cameras use visual imaging to identify obstacles, ultrasound sends out sound waves and measures their reflection to determine distances, and LiDAR uses laser beams to create 3D maps of its surroundings. Each type of sensor has its own strengths and weaknesses, making them suitable for different applications in industries such as automotive, industrial automation, and robotics.</p></p>
