<p><p>EO gimbals are widely used in various industries for stabilizing cameras and sensors. There are two main types of EO gimbals based on the number of axes they support: 2-axis and 3-axis gimbals. 2-axis gimbals stabilize the camera or sensor along two axes, typically pitch and roll, providing basic stabilization. On the other hand, 3-axis gimbals stabilize the camera or sensor along three axes, including pitch, roll, and yaw, offering more advanced and precise stabilization for capturing high-quality images and videos.</p></p>
