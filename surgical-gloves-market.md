<p><p>Surgical gloves are classified into two main types- natural latex and synthetic. Natural latex surgical gloves are made from natural rubber latex and provide excellent dexterity and flexibility while ensuring a secure grip. Synthetic surgical gloves are made from materials like nitrile, neoprene, or polyisoprene, offering an alternative for individuals with latex allergies. These gloves are durable, puncture-resistant, and provide a high level of sensitivity and protection during surgical procedures. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining a sterile environment and preventing cross-contamination in medical settings.</p></p>
