<p><ul><li>Semi-active Suspension System</li><li>Active Suspension System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Adaptive Suspension System market consists of two main types: Semi-active Suspension System and Active Suspension System. Semi-active Suspension System uses electronically controlled dampers to adjust the suspension stiffness based on driving conditions, providing a balance of comfort and performance. Active Suspension System, on the other hand, features hydraulic actuators that actively control the suspension in real-time, providing superior ride quality and handling. Both systems offer improved stability, comfort, and performance, catering to the varying needs of drivers in the automotive market.</p></p>
