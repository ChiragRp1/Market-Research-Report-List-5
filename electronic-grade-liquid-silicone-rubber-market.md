<p><p>Electronic Grade Liquid Silicone Rubber (LSR) is available in two main types - Single Component LSR and Two Component LSR. Single Component LSR is a ready-to-use material that requires no mixing before application, making it convenient and easy to use. On the other hand, Two Component LSR consists of two separate components that need to be mixed together before use, allowing for more precise control over the material properties. Both types are commonly used in the electronics industry for applications such as gaskets, seals, and insulation.</p></p>
