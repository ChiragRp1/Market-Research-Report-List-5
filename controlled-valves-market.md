<p><ul><li>Pneumatic Controlled Valves</li><li>Electric Controlled Valves</li><li>Hydraulic Controlled Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Controlled valves are categorized into three main types based on their mechanism of operation: Pneumatic Controlled Valves, Electric Controlled Valves, and Hydraulic Controlled Valves. Pneumatic Controlled Valves are operated using compressed air, Electric Controlled Valves are controlled by an electric signal, and Hydraulic Controlled Valves use hydraulic fluid to regulate flow. Each type of valve offers unique advantages and is used in various industries, such as manufacturing, oil and gas, and agriculture, depending on their specific requirements and applications.</p></p>
