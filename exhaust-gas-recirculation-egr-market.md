<p><p>Exhaust Gas Recirculation (EGR) systems help reduce emissions and improve fuel efficiency in vehicles by recirculating a portion of the exhaust gas back into the engine. There are two main types of EGR coolers used in these systems - Tube EGR Coolers and Finned EGR Coolers. Tube EGR Coolers use a series of tubes to cool the recirculated exhaust gas, while Finned EGR Coolers use fins to increase the surface area for better heat dissipation. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining engine performance and reducing harmful emissions.</p></p>
