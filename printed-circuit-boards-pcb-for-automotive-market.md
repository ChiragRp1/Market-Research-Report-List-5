<p><p>Printed Circuit Boards (PCB) for Automotive market includes Single Layer Rigid PCB, Multilayer Rigid PCB, and Flexible PCB types. Single Layer Rigid PCB consists of a single layer of substrate material with conductive copper traces. Multilayer Rigid PCB contains multiple layers of substrate material with interconnected copper traces. Flexible PCB is made of flexible substrate material allowing it to bend or flex during use. These different types cater to various automotive applications requiring specific design and functionality.</p></p>
