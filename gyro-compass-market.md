<p><p>Gyro compasses are navigational devices used to determine the direction of true north. There are two main types of gyro compasses: fiber optic gyro compasses and mechanical gyro compasses. Fiber optic gyro compasses use a laser light to measure the rotation of the Earth, providing accurate readings even in harsh environments. On the other hand, mechanical gyro compasses rely on spinning mechanical parts to detect and maintain the correct direction. Both types of gyro compasses are essential for ensuring accurate navigation at sea.</p></p>
