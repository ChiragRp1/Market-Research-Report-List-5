The Bridge Support system utilizes a bridge structure to support the equipment needed for sludge removal. The Column Support system uses vertical columns to support the equipment. The Traction system involves using a motorized mechanism to move the equipment along the clarifier tank. Each system offers unique advantages and is suitable for different types of clarification processes.</p></p>
