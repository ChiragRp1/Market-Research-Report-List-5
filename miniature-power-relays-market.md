<p><p>Miniature Power Relays are categorized into AC Power and DC Power markets based on the type of power source they are designed to work with. AC Power relays are used in applications where the power source is alternating current, typically found in household and industrial electrical systems. On the other hand, DC Power relays are designed for use with direct current, commonly found in automotive, telecommunications, and renewable energy systems. Both types of relays are compact in size and high in performance, making them suitable for various electronic and electrical applications.</p></p>
