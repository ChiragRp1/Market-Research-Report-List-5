<p><p>Phase Only Spatial Light Modulators (SLMs) can be classified into two main types based on the presence of dielectric mirrors. Dielectric Mirror Type SLMs utilize mirrors made of dielectric materials to manipulate the phase of light. On the other hand, No-Dielectric Mirror Type SLMs do not have dielectric mirrors and may rely on alternative mechanisms for phase modulation. Both types are used in various applications such as holography, optical encryption, and adaptive optics, with each offering its own advantages and limitations.</p></p>
