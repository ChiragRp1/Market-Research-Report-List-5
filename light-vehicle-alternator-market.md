<p><ul><li>Salient Pole Type</li><li>Smooth Cylindrical Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Light Vehicle Alternator Market includes two types - Salient Pole Type and Smooth Cylindrical Type. Salient Pole Type alternators have a design with protruding electromagnets on the rotor. They are commonly used in larger vehicles and heavy machinery due to their higher starting torque. Smooth Cylindrical Type alternators have a smoother rotor design and are typically used in light vehicles for their compact size and efficiency. Both types play a crucial role in generating electricity to power the vehicle's electrical systems.</p></p>
