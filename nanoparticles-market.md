Polymeric Nanoparticles are made of synthetic polymers for controlled drug delivery. Dendrimers are highly structured polymers used for drug delivery and imaging. Inorganic Nanoparticles are made of metals or metal oxides for various applications. Others category includes a variety of nanoparticles with unique properties and uses.</p></p>
