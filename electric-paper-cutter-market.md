<p><ul><li>Semi-automatic</li><li>Automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric paper cutters come in two main types: semi-automatic and automatic. Semi-automatic paper cutters require manual input for setting the cutting parameters and placing the paper, but the cutting process itself is automated. On the other hand, automatic paper cutters are fully electronic with features like programmed cutting sequences and automatic alignment. While semi-automatic cutters are more common in small-scale operations, automatic cutters are preferred for larger-scale businesses due to their efficiency and productivity.</p></p>
