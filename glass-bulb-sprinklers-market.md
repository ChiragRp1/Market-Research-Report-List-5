<p><p>Glass bulb sprinklers are categorized into different types based on their installation position and application. Pendent sprinklers are typically installed hanging down from the ceiling, while upright sprinklers are mounted in an upright position. Horizontal sidewall sprinklers are installed on the sidewall of a room, providing coverage in a specific direction. Concealed sprinklers are hidden within the ceiling and only become visible when activated. Each type of sprinkler is designed to provide efficient and effective fire protection in various settings.</p></p>
