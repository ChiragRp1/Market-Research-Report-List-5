These cleaners are ideal for larger pools with complex shapes and uneven surfaces. On the other hand, Wheel Drive robotic pool cleaners use rotating wheels to navigate the pool, making them more suitable for smaller pools with straightforward shapes and smooth surfaces. Both types of cleaners are effective in removing dirt, debris, and algae from the pool, providing hassle-free cleaning solutions for pool owners.</p></p>
