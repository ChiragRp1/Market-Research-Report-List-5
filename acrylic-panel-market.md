<p><p>Acrylic panels are widely used in various industries, with two main types dominating the market: extruded acrylic panels and cast acrylic panels. Extruded acrylic panels are produced through a process of pushing liquid acrylic through a die, resulting in a product with consistent thickness and lower cost. On the other hand, cast acrylic panels are made by pouring liquid acrylic into molds and letting it set, producing a higher quality product with better optical clarity and scratch resistance, but at a higher cost.</p></p>
