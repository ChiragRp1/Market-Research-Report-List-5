<p><strong>The Semiconductor Wafer Electrostatic Chucks ESC Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor wafer electrostatic chucks (ESCs) are categorized into two main types: Coulomb Type and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type. </p><p>Coulomb Type ESCs utilize the attractive force between opposite electrical charges to hold the wafer in place. On the other hand, JR Type ESCs operate based on the generation of an electrostatic force due to the presence of a dielectric layer between the chuck and the wafer. Each type has its own advantages and limitations, making them suitable for different applications in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
