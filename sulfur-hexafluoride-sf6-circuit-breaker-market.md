<p><p>SF6 circuit breakers are available in two main types based on their structure: Porcelain Pole Structure and Tank-type Structure. The Porcelain Pole Structure features a compact design with all components housed in a porcelain enclosure mounted on a pole, making it suitable for outdoor applications. Both types offer reliable operation and efficient interrupting performance in electrical systems.</p></p>
