<p><ul><li>Thermal Slide－off Debonding</li><li>Mechanical Debonding</li><li>Laser Debonding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Temporary bonding adhesive market types include thermal slide-off debonding, mechanical debonding, and laser debonding. Thermal slide-off debonding involves heating the adhesive to release the bond. Mechanical debonding requires physical force to break the bond. Laser debonding utilizes laser energy to break down the adhesive bond. Each method offers unique benefits depending on the application, providing flexibility and ease of use for various industries such as electronics, automotive, and medical devices.</p></p>
