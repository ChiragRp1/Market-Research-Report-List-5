<p><p>Glass Fiber Reinforced Plastic (GFRP) composite materials are divided into two main types based on the type of plastic used: thermosetting plastic products and thermoplastic plastic products. Thermosetting plastic products are materials that become permanently hardened after being heated, making them durable and resistant to high temperatures. On the other hand, thermoplastic plastic products can be melted and reshaped multiple times, offering flexibility and easy processing. Both types of GFRP composite materials have unique properties that make them suitable for various applications in different industries.</p></p>
