Each material offers unique characteristics suitable for different applications. PP tubes are widely used due to their chemical resistance, while PC tubes are known for their durability. PS tubes are transparent and have good clarity, while PF and CAB tubes offer chemical resistance and high heat resistance respectively.</p></p>
