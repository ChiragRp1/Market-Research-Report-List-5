They are typically used in buildings where permanent connections can be established. On the other hand, wireless intercom systems use radio signals to transmit information, allowing for greater mobility and flexibility. They are commonly used in settings where wiring installation is not feasible or cost-effective. Both wired and wireless intercom systems offer reliable communication solutions, but the choice between them depends on the specific needs and limitations of the installation environment.</p></p>
