<p><strong>The IP Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centralized IP Camera</li><li>Decentralized IP Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Centralized IP Camera market involves cameras that require a central network video recorder (NVR) for recording and storage. These cameras send video data to the NVR for processing and management. On the other hand, Decentralized IP Camera market consists of cameras that have built-in recording capabilities, eliminating the need for a separate NVR. These cameras can store and manage video data locally. Centralized cameras offer centralized control and management, while decentralized cameras provide flexibility and cost savings on NVR equipment. Both markets cater to different surveillance needs and preferences.</p></p>
