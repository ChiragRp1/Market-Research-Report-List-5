Ceramics substrates provide superior thermal stability and electrical insulating properties, ideal for power modules and LEDs. Glass substrates are preferred for their excellent chemical resistance and low dielectric constant, making them suitable for high-frequency applications. Each type caters to different requirements in the semiconductor industry, providing a range of options for packaging solutions.</p></p>
