<p><p>Natural Hydraulic Lime (NHL) is classified into three main categories based on their compressive strength: NHL2, NHL3.5, and NHL5. NHL2 has the lowest compressive strength, suitable for interior plaster and non-load-bearing applications. NHL3.5 is a versatile option for both interior and exterior projects, offering moderate strength. NHL5 is the strongest type, ideal for high-stress environments like structural masonry. Each NHL type caters to different construction needs, providing varying levels of strength and durability.</p></p>
