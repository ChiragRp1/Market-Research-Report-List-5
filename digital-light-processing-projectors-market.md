<p><p>Digital Light Processing projectors are categorized into three types based on the number of DLP chips used in the projection system. 1-Chip Type projectors use a single DLP chip to produce images, offering cost-effective solutions for home theaters and small businesses. 2-Chips Type projectors utilize two DLP chips to enhance color accuracy and image quality, making them suitable for professional applications. 3-Chips Type projectors employ three DLP chips for superior color reproduction and image clarity, commonly found in high-end home theaters and large venues.</p></p>
