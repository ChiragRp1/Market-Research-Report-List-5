<p><p>The Smart Sorting System market is divided into two main types: Linear Sorting Systems and Loop Sorting Systems. Linear Sorting Systems consist of conveyor belts that move products in a straight line for sorting, while Loop Sorting Systems use a looped track to continuously circulate items for sorting. Both systems are designed to automate the sorting process in warehouses and distribution centers, increasing efficiency and accuracy. These technologies are in demand as businesses seek to streamline their operations and meet growing consumer demands for faster delivery times.</p></p>
