<p><p>Effervescent tablet packaging is available in two main types: tube packaging and foil wrapping. Tube packaging involves storing effervescent tablets in a cylindrical container made of materials such as plastic or cardboard, providing a convenient and easy-to-use format for consumers. On the other hand, foil wrapping involves individually wrapping each tablet in aluminum foil, which offers better protection against moisture and UV light. Both packaging types are commonly used in the market to ensure the quality and effectiveness of effervescent tablets.</p></p>
