<p><p>Cloud Block Storage market consists of two main types: Locally-attached Storage and Network-attached Storage. Locally-attached Storage is directly connected to a single server, offering high performance and low latency for specific applications. On the other hand, Network-attached Storage allows multiple servers to access the storage over a network, providing scalability and flexibility for shared resources. Both types of storage play a crucial role in enabling organizations to store and manage their data effectively in the cloud environment.</p></p>
