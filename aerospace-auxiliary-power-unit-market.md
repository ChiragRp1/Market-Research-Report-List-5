<p><p>The Aerospace Auxiliary Power Unit market includes two main types: Shaft power output type and Compressed air output type. Shaft power output units are designed to provide mechanical power to drive various aircraft systems, such as hydraulic pumps and generators. On the other hand, Compressed air output units generate high-pressure air for starting aircraft engines and operating pneumatic systems. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring the efficient and safe operation of aircraft systems and are essential components in the aerospace industry.</p></p>
