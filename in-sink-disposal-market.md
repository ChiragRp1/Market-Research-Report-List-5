<p><strong>The In-sink Disposal Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Batch Feed Type</li><li>Continuous Feed Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The in-sink disposal market is categorized into two main types: batch feed and continuous feed systems. Batch feed disposals require the user to fill the chamber before grinding, providing a safer option as it only operates when the cover is securely in place. Continuous feed disposals, on the other hand, can continuously grind waste while the unit is running. Both types offer convenience and efficiency in disposing of food waste, but batch feed disposals are typically preferred for their added safety features.</p></p>
