<p><p>Radiation Detection Products market can be categorized into two main types: Dose Detection and Non-dose Detection. Dose Detection refers to products that are designed to measure and quantify the amount of radiation exposure an individual or environment has received. On the other hand, Non-dose Detection products focus on detecting the presence of radiation without providing specific dose information. Both types of products play a crucial role in various industries such as healthcare, nuclear power, defense, and research to ensure safety and monitoring of radiation levels.</p></p>
