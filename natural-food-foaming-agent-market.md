<p><p>Natural food foaming agents are available in both solid and liquid forms in the market. Solid foaming agents are typically in the form of powders or granules, which require dissolution in water before use. On the other hand, liquid foaming agents are pre-dissolved and ready-to-use, making them easier to incorporate into food products. While solid foaming agents are more commonly used in baking applications, liquid foaming agents are preferred in beverages and sauces for their convenience and consistency.</p></p>
