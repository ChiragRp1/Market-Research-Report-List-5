<p><p>Parcel Sortation Systems market includes Horizontal Cross Strip Sorter and Vertical Cross Strip Sorter. Horizontal Cross Strip Sorter system enables parcels to be sorted efficiently by moving them on a flat surface with horizontal cross strips diverting them to the correct chute. Vertical Cross Strip Sorter, on the other hand, utilizes a vertical conveyor system with cross strips diverting parcels to the designated chute. Both systems are essential for streamlining parcel sorting processes in logistics and e-commerce industries.</p></p>
