<p><ul><li>MCU</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Embedded Wi-Fi modules are commonly divided into two market types: MCU (Microcontroller Unit) and Other market. MCU modules integrate both the Wi-Fi chip and the microcontroller on a single chip, simplifying the design process and reducing costs. Other market modules typically involve separate Wi-Fi chips that are connected to external microcontrollers, providing more flexibility in terms of customization and performance. Both types of modules cater to different needs and preferences in the IoT and connected device industry.</p></p>
