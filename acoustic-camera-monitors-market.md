<p><p>Acoustic camera monitors come in two main types: handheld/tablet type and microphone array type. Handheld/tablet type monitors are portable, easy to use, and allow for quick acoustic analysis of noise sources. On the other hand, microphone array type monitors feature multiple microphones positioned in an array, enabling detailed sound localization and visualization. Both types of monitors serve the same purpose of monitoring and analyzing sound sources, but offer different levels of portability and precision in their acoustic measurements.</p></p>
