<p><p>Bulk Acoustic Wave Sensors refer to devices that utilize the propagation of acoustic waves to measure various physical quantities. There are two main types in the market - Quartz Crystal Microbalance (QCM) or Thickness Shear Mode (TSM) sensors and Film Bulk Acoustic Resonator (FBAR) or Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) sensors. QCM-TSM sensors measure mass changes on the sensor surface, while FBAR-SMR sensors detect changes in frequency due to mass loading. Both types offer high sensitivity and are commonly used in various applications such as gas sensing, biosensing, and telecommunications.</p></p>
