Linear Sortation Systems are designed with a straight line conveyor layout, allowing for precise sorting of items at high speeds. Loop Sortation Systems utilize a circular or looped conveyor layout, enabling continuous motion of products for sorting. Both systems offer benefits such as increased productivity, reduced labor costs, and enhanced accuracy in order fulfillment, making them essential for modern industrial operations.</p></p>
