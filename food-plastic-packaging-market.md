<p><p>Food plastic packaging market types can be classified into three categories based on the type of polymers used - Thermoset Polymer, Thermoplastic Polymer, and Elastomer. Thermoset polymers are rigid and chemically resistant, making them suitable for durable packaging. Thermoplastic polymers are flexible and easy to mold, allowing for various shapes and sizes of packaging. Elastomers have elastic properties and are used for materials that require stretchability. Each type has unique characteristics that cater to different packaging needs in the food industry.</p></p>
