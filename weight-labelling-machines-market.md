<p><p>Weight labeling machines are classified into two main market types: manual type and automatic type. Manual type machines require human operation to input data and affix labels on products, making them suitable for smaller-scale operations. On the other hand, automatic type machines are equipped with advanced technology that allows for automated label application and data input, making them ideal for larger-scale production facilities. Both types of machines serve the same purpose of accurately labeling products based on their weight, but differ in terms of operation and scale of production.</p></p>
