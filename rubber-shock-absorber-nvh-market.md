<p><p>Rubber shock absorbers for NVH (Noise, Vibration, Harshness) can be categorized into three main types: Engine NVH, Chassis NVH, and Others. Engine NVH shock absorbers are designed to reduce vibrations and noise coming from the engine, improving overall driving experience. Chassis NVH shock absorbers focus on minimizing vibrations and noise from the vehicle's chassis, resulting in a smoother ride. The Others category includes shock absorbers that target specific areas of NVH such as suspension systems or interior components.</p></p>
