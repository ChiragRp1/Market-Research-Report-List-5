<p><ul><li>Liner Polywoven Bags</li><li>Non-Liner Polywoven Bags</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polywoven bags come in two main types based on their construction: liner polywoven bags and non-liner polywoven bags. Liner polywoven bags have an additional layer inside the bag to provide extra protection from moisture and contamination, making them ideal for storing food or chemicals. On the other hand, non-liner polywoven bags do not have this extra layer, making them more suitable for dry goods or products that do not require as much protection. Both types of polywoven bags are durable, reusable, and cost-effective packaging solutions for various industries.</p></p>
