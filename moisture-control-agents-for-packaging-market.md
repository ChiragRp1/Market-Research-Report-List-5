<p><p>Moisture control agents are substances used in packaging to absorb or adsorb moisture, preventing damage to products. Montmorillonite clay, silica gel, molecular sieve, calcium oxide, and calcium sulfate are common types. Montmorillonite clay and silica gel are natural substances that absorb moisture effectively. Molecular sieves have uniform pores to selectively adsorb water molecules. Calcium oxide and calcium sulfate are commonly used desiccants that absorb moisture through chemical reactions. Each type offers unique benefits for moisture control in packaging applications.</p></p>
