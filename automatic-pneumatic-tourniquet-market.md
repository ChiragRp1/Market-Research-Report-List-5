<p><ul><li>Single Channel</li><li>Dual Channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Pneumatic Tourniquet market includes two main types: Single Channel and Dual Channel tourniquets. Single Channel tourniquets have one pressure channel, making them suitable for use in procedures on a single limb. On the other hand, Dual Channel tourniquets have two pressure channels, allowing for simultaneous inflation and deflation of multiple cuffs. This makes Dual Channel tourniquets more versatile and efficient in surgeries involving multiple limbs or body parts. Both types offer advanced pneumatic technology for controlling blood flow during medical procedures.</p></p>
