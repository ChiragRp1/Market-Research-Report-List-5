<p><p>Patient monitors play a crucial role in modern healthcare, with a variety of types available to monitor different aspects of a patient's health. Pulse oximeters measure oxygen saturation levels in the blood, spirometers monitor lung function, capnographs measure carbon dioxide levels in exhaled breath, and peak flow meters assess the speed at which air is exhaled from the lungs. These devices provide valuable information to healthcare providers, allowing for timely interventions and improved patient care. The market for these devices continues to grow as the demand for accurate and efficient patient monitoring increases.</p></p>
