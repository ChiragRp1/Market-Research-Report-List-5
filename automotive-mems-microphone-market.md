<p><p>Automotive MEMS Microphone market consists of two primary types: Analog Microphone and Digital Microphone. Analog Microphones convert sound waves into electrical signals, which are then processed by analog circuits. On the other hand, Digital Microphones convert sound waves into digital signals using an analog-to-digital converter, allowing for direct processing by digital devices. Both types of microphones play a crucial role in automotive applications, providing high-quality audio input for features such as hands-free calling, voice recognition, and active noise cancellation.</p></p>
