<p><p>Agricultural crop insurance can typically be categorized into two main types: Multi-Peril Crop Insurance (MPCI) and Hail Insurance. MPCI provides coverage for a variety of risks such as weather events, disease, and pests that can damage crops. Hail insurance specifically protects against damage caused by hail storms. Both types of insurance are important for farmers to mitigate their financial risks and ensure stability in their crop production. Farmers can choose the type of insurance that best suits their needs and the specific risks they face in their region.</p></p>
