Instead, they rely on desiccant material to adsorb moisture from the compressed air, resulting in dry and clean air. These dryers are commonly used in industries where moisture can impact the quality of products or affect the performance of pneumatic equipment.</p><p>The Heatless Air Dryers Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.3% during the forecast period.
