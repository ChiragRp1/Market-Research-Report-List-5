<p><ul><li>ABL</li><li>PBL</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cosmetics laminate tube packaging market types include ABL (aluminum barrier laminate) and PBL (plastic barrier laminate). ABL tubes are made of multiple layers of aluminum and plastic, providing excellent barrier properties and protection against external factors. PBL tubes, on the other hand, are made of various layers of plastic, offering flexibility in design and customization options. Both ABL and PBL tubes are widely used in the cosmetics industry for products such as creams, lotions, and gels due to their durability and aesthetic appeal.</p></p>
