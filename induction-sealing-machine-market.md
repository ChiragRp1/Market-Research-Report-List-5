<p><ul><li>Manual Type</li><li>Automatic Type</li><li>Semi-automotive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Induction sealing machines are divided into three main types based on their operation: manual type, automatic type, and semi-automatic type. Manual induction sealing machines require the operator to place the cap on the container and activate the sealing process. Automatic induction sealing machines operate without human intervention, sealing containers in a continuous process. Semi-automatic machines require some manual input, such as placing the cap on the container, but automate the majority of the sealing process. These different types cater to varying production needs and volumes in the packaging industry.</p></p>
