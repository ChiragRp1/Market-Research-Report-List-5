<p><strong>The Handheld Laser Scanner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SCSI Interface</li><li>EPP Interface</li><li>USB Interface</li><li>PS2 Interface</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Handheld laser scanners come in four main types based on their interface options: SCSI, EPP, USB, and PS2. SCSI interface scanners are known for their high data transfer rates and compatibility with various computer systems. EPP interface scanners offer enhanced parallel port connectivity for faster data transfer. USB interface scanners are the most common and offer simple plug-and-play functionality. PS2 interface scanners are designed for older systems that may not have USB ports. Each type offers unique advantages depending on the user's needs and system requirements.</p></p>
