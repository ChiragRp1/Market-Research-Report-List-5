<p><p>Torque analyzers are available in two main types in the market - manual type and electric type. Electric torque analyzers, on the other hand, use an electric motor to measure torque, making them more accurate and suitable for higher torque applications. Both types of torque analyzers are widely used across industries for quality control, assembly line testing, and other torque measurement applications.</p></p>
