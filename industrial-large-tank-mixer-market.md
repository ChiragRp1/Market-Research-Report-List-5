<p><p>Industrial large tank mixers come in three main types: top mounted, side mounted, and bottom mounted. Top mounted mixers are installed on the lid of the tank and are ideal for mixing large volumes of liquid. Side mounted mixers are attached vertically to the side of the tank and are suitable for applications that require thorough mixing. Bottom mounted mixers are installed at the bottom of the tank and are used for applications where solid particles need to be suspended or dissolved in the liquid.</p></p>
