<p><strong>The Plastic Sorting Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chute-Type Sorting Machine</li><li>Belt-Type Sorting Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plastic sorting machines are categorized into chute-type sorting machines and belt-type sorting machines. Chute-type sorting machines utilize gravity to separate plastics based on their weight and size, making them suitable for smaller items. On the other hand, belt-type sorting machines use a conveyor belt to transport plastics through sensors that detect and separate different materials based on their properties. Both types of machines play a crucial role in recycling facilities by efficiently sorting plastics for further processing.</p></p>
