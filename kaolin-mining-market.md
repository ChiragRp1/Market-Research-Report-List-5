<p><ul><li>Water-washed</li><li>Airfloat</li><li>Calcined</li><li>Delaminated</li><li>Surface-modified & Unprocessed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Kaolin mining market types include water-washed, airfloat, calcined, delaminated, surface-modified, and unprocessed kaolin. Water-washed kaolin is processed by washing to remove impurities, while airfloat kaolin undergoes a refining process to achieve a fine particle size. Calcined kaolin is heated to improve brightness and opacity, while delaminated kaolin is processed to separate into thin layers. Surface-modified kaolin is chemically treated for specific properties, and unprocessed kaolin is raw material directly from the mine.</p></p>
