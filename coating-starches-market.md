<p><p>Coating starches are used in various industries for different purposes. Oxidized starch is used for improving adhesion and binding properties in coatings. Cross-linked starch provides enhanced stability and resistance to shear forces. Hydroxyalkyl starch offers increased film-forming properties. Porous starch is known for its high absorption capacity and improved texture in coatings. Each type of coating starch serves a specific function based on its unique properties, making them essential ingredients in the formulation of various coatings.</p></p>
