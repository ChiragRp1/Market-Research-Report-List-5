<p><p>Multistage cylinders are commonly divided into two main types: single-acting cylinders and double-acting cylinders. Single-acting cylinders use hydraulic pressure to extend the piston, but rely on an external force (such as gravity or a spring) to retract it. On the other hand, double-acting cylinders utilize hydraulic pressure to both extend and retract the piston, providing more control and precision in various applications. Both types are widely used in industrial and engineering sectors for a variety of tasks.</p></p>
