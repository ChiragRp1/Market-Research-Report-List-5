<p><p>Mains Distribution Units (MDU) include Basic MDUs, Metered MDUs, Monitored MDUs, and Switched MDUs. Basic MDUs provide simple power distribution without any additional features. Metered MDUs measure energy consumption at each outlet for billing purposes. Monitored MDUs allow remote monitoring and management of power usage. Switched MDUs include the ability to remotely turn outlets on and off, providing control over connected devices. Each type of MDU caters to different requirements and usage scenarios in various market segments.</p></p>
