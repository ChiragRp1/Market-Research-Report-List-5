<p><ul><li>Toggle MRAM</li><li>STT-MRAM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Magnetoresistive Random Access Memory (MRAM) includes two main types: Toggle MRAM and STT-MRAM. Toggle MRAM utilizes a toggling mechanism to switch between memory states, offering fast and efficient performance. On the other hand, STT-MRAM technology uses spin-transfer torque to read and write data, providing lower power consumption and higher density. Both types have shown potential for use in automotive applications due to their non-volatile nature, high speed, and reliability. These advancements in MRAM technology are expected to drive growth in the automotive MRAM market.</p></p>
