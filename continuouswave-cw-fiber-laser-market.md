<p><ul><li>Single Mode</li><li>Multi Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Continuous wave (CW) fiber lasers are available in two main market types: single mode and multi mode. </p><p>Single mode fiber lasers emit a laser beam in a single spatial mode, resulting in a more focused and precise beam with high coherence. These lasers are ideal for applications requiring high beam quality, such as telecommunications and material processing.</p><p>Multi mode fiber lasers emit a laser beam in multiple spatial modes, providing higher power and a larger beam diameter. These lasers are commonly used in industrial cutting, welding, and drilling applications.</p></p>
