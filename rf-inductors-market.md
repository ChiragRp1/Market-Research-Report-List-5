<p><strong>The RF Inductors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wire Wound RF Inductors</li><li>Film RF Inductors</li><li>Multilayer RF Inductors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RF inductors are essential components in RF circuit design, used for impedance matching, filtering, and energy storage. There are three main types of RF inductors on the market: wire wound RF inductors, film RF inductors, and multilayer RF inductors. Wire wound inductors are constructed using a wire wrapped around a core, providing high inductance values. Film inductors use a thin film deposited on a substrate, offering lower losses and higher Q values. Multilayer inductors are constructed using multiple layers of conductive material, providing high inductance in a compact size.</p></p>
