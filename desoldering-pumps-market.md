<p><p>Desoldering pumps are tools used to remove solder from electronic components. Plunger type pumps have a spring-loaded plunger that creates a vacuum when released, sucking up molten solder. Bulb type pumps use a rubber bulb to create suction by squeezing and releasing it. Both types are popular in the market for their effectiveness in desoldering. Plunger type pumps are preferred for their precise control, while bulb type pumps are known for their simplicity and ease of use.</p></p>
