<p><p>Quarter-turn actuators are used to automate the operation of valves by providing a quarter-turn motion. There are three main types of quarter-turn actuators: electric, pneumatic, and hydraulic. Electric quarter-turn actuators are powered by electricity, providing precise control and automation. Pneumatic quarter-turn actuators use compressed air to operate the valve, offering quick and efficient performance. Hydraulic quarter-turn actuators use hydraulic fluid to control the valve, suitable for high-pressure applications. Each type of quarter-turn actuator offers unique advantages depending on the specific needs of the valve system.</p></p>
