<p><p>Magnetron sputtering sources are categorized into two main types based on their operating conditions: HV (High Vacuum) and UHV (Ultra High Vacuum) magnetron sputtering sources. HV magnetron sputtering sources are suitable for applications requiring medium to high vacuum levels, while UHV magnetron sputtering sources are designed for ultra-clean environments with extremely low pressure levels. Both types of sources are commonly used in thin film deposition processes in industries such as semiconductors, electronics, and optics.</p></p>
