<p><strong>The Polyimide Tubing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Seamless Tubing</li><li>Spiral Wound Tubing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyimide tubing is available in two main types - seamless tubing and spiral wound tubing. Seamless tubing is manufactured without any seams or joints, providing a smooth and uniform surface for various industrial applications. On the other hand, spiral wound tubing is constructed by winding multiple layers of polyimide tape in a spiral pattern, offering enhanced flexibility and strength. Both types of tubing are commonly used in industries such as aerospace, electronics, and medical devices for their high temperature resistance and excellent electrical insulation properties.</p></p>
