<p><p>Automotive Brake Friction Product Market includes three main types: Brake Pads, Brake Shoes, and Brake Lining. Brake pads are the most common type made of friction material pressed against the brake rotor to slow down or stop the vehicle. Brake shoes are used in drum brake systems, pressing against the drum to create friction. Brake lining is a component found in both pads and shoes, providing the necessary friction to stop the vehicle. Each type plays a crucial role in ensuring safe and efficient braking performance.</p></p>
