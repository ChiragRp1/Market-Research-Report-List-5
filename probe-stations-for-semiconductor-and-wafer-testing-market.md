<p><p>Probe stations for semiconductor and wafer testing come in three main types: manual, semi-automated, and automated. Manual probe stations require operators to physically adjust the position of probes on the wafer, making them the most time-consuming but also the most affordable option. Semi-automated probe stations use motorized stages to move probes, reducing operator error and increasing efficiency. Automated probe stations are the most advanced, utilizing robotic arms and software control to fully automate the testing process, offering the highest level of accuracy and throughput.</p></p>
