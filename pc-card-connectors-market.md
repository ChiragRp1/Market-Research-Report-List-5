<p><p>PC Card Connectors come in two main types: Type I and Type II. Type I connectors measure 3.3mm in height and are designed for memory cards. They are commonly used in laptops and other portable devices. On the other hand, Type II connectors are thicker, measuring 5.5mm in height, and are used for input/output cards. Both Type I and Type II connectors allow for easy and secure insertion of PC cards into electronic devices, providing a reliable and efficient connection.</p></p>
