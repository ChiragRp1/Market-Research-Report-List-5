<p><p>Motion sickness treatment market consists of various types, including Anticholinergic, Antihistamines, and Others. Anticholinergic medications work by blocking the action of acetylcholine in the brain, reducing symptoms of nausea and vomiting. Antihistamines help to control symptoms by blocking histamine receptors in the brain. Other treatments may include prescription medications, such as scopolamine patches, or natural remedies like ginger or acupressure bands. These different types of treatments provide options for individuals suffering from motion sickness to find relief.</p></p>
