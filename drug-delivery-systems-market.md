<p><p>Drug Delivery Systems market is categorized into various types based on the mechanism of delivering drugs to the targeted site. Intrauterine implants are used for long-term drug delivery in women. Prodrug implants involve inactive compounds that are converted into active drugs inside the body. Polymeric drug delivery systems use biocompatible polymers to release drugs slowly over time. Targeted drug delivery systems deliver medication directly to specific cells or tissues. Other types include nano-carriers, liposomes, and micelles for precise drug delivery.</p></p>
