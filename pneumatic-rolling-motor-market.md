<p><p>Pneumatic Rolling Motors can be categorized into two main types based on torque control – Torque Controlled Motors and Stall Torque Motors. Torque Controlled Motors allow for precise control of the motor's torque output, making them ideal for applications where accurate torque management is essential. On the other hand, Stall Torque Motors are designed to provide maximum torque output at low speeds, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications that require high starting torque. Each type offers unique advantages depending on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
